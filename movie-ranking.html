The Best Webseries According to Me

My Top 3 Favourites

Indian Police Force

A high-action series based on terrorism and cross-border conflict. The story focuses on how the police tackle dangerous threats to the nation.

The Family Man

A brilliantly written show about an intelligence officer balancing secret missions and family responsibilities while serving the country.

Mirzapur

A gritty crime drama centered around power, politics, and the violent struggle to rule Mirzapur.
